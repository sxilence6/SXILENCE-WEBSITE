## What this is
A static portfolio/archive site for Matteo Ruggiero / sxilence, collecting works, sound, performances, press, external traces, and project pages in raw HTML/CSS/JS.

## Why it exists / emotional core
To hold the work as a broken machine log rather than a clean portfolio: crash logic, avatars, cars, damaged devices, music, body traces, and public web residue stay visibly alive.

## Who it's for
Visitors, curators, venues, collaborators, and people following the sxilence/Matteo work who need a navigable archive without killing the weird signal.

## Mood & direction
Dark, image-heavy, compressed, glitchy, lower-case, tactile, and a little unstable. The site should feel like a system menu, recovered hard drive, stage light, and haunted broadcast sharing one body.

## Non-goals
- Not a generic clean white artist portfolio.
- Not a startup-style personal brand page.
- Not a heavy framework project unless the raw static structure becomes a real blocker.
- Not an archive that explains every strange thing until it becomes harmless.
