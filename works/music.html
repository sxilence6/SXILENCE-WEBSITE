The writing on Opravit se is associative, more soundscape than straight lyrics. It sits in the darker side of growing up online: social media addiction and the pull to stay connected that's been there since childhood.

Sound-wise it pulls from club music, post-rap and crystalline sound design, landing somewhere between pop and experimental electronica.
