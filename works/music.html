The lyricism of Opravit se is associative and soundscape-like. It explores the shadowy sides of postinternet reality, especially the addiction to social media and the consuming desire to stay online since early childhood.

Sonically, it takes inspiration from global club music, post-rap and crystalline sound design, creating an evocative dark world at the intersection of pop and experimental electronica.
