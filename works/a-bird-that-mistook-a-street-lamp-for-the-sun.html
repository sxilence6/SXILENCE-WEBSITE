How do we swim in a world drowning in images? Using void narration and methods of hyperlink cinema, the work asks what kind of sense-making is possible in times when we feel it slipping away.

In the form of a simple decision-making visual novel, you join a bird that mistakes the glow of a streetlamp for the Sun. Under overexposure, your navigation breaks, glitching through a speculative landscape and the anatomy of pain itself.

The body is a praying machine. Together with the bird, you meet entities, ghosts of unlived futures, whispers of geological trauma, and the fragility inside planetary bones. Deep time, catastrophism, and bodies of dust.
