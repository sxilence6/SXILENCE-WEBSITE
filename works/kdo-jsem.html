Kdo jsem? [Who am I?] is a deeply personal video where I explore my own identity through the character of Matteo, who is a direct representation of myself. The surreal narrative becomes an introspective journey of breaking free from societal constraints and finding authentic self-expression.

Looking back, each frame captured an internal struggle to escape the systemic expectations that had been constraining me. Matteo's transformation, symbolized by the meeting of the ant, represents an awakening to the possibility of living true to the inner voice.

The 3D animation was intentionally degraded through a CRT television. The damage amplifies the anxiety of feeling trapped, turning the image into a raw visual metaphor of the emotional landscape.
