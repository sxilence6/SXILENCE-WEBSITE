The App Whisperer is a modular performance that keeps changing over time. It is not a fixed piece, but a system that grows, breaks, mutates and transforms through each version.

Different instruments, devices, objects and concepts are added or removed depending on the moment, the space and the current state of the work. The performance is based on the act of trying to open a portal.

The portal is not only a fictional or sci-fi image, but also a way of thinking about performance itself: something that can happen only in the present moment. Through sound, movement, physical devices and instructions, the body, machine and situation begin to communicate with each other.

It is about instability, attention and transformation. A live process where failure, repetition and improvisation become part of the meaning. The work is always becoming something else.
